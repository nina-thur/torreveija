Torrevieja lies about 50 kilometres south of the city of Alicante.
Torrevieja was originally a salt-mining and fishing village as it is located
between the sea and two large salt lakes (Las Salinas, a pink lake),
which gives Torrevieja healthy microclimate.

Did you know that Torrevieja has 320 days of sunshine per year?
